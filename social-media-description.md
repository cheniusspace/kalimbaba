**Full description**
```
Kalimbaba brings the joy of kalimba music to your fingertips.

Whether you're picking up the thumb piano for the first time or you're
a seasoned player looking for new songs to learn, Kalimbaba has you covered.

FEATURES
- Browse a curated catalog of kalimba tabs
- Filter by genre, difficulty, and audience
- Follow along note by note with our elegant tab viewer
- Save your favorite songs for quick access
- Dark & light mode — looks beautiful either way
- Works seamlessly on web and mobile

HOW IT WORKS
Each tab shows you exactly which keys to pluck, with note names and
syllables displayed in perfect sync — so you can focus on the music,
not the mechanics.

Designed with love for the kalimba community.
```
